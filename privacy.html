The legal basis for processing is any of the following:

  1. Consent: This will apply where you have provided your consent to the processing of your Personal Data for one or more specific purposes;
  2. Performance of Contract:  This will apply where processing is necessary for the performance of a contract to which you are a party or to take steps before entering into a contract with you;
  3. Vital interests: This is where processing is required for the protection of your vital interests.
  4. Compliance with legal obligation: Where we are subject to a legal obligation and need to use our Personal Data to comply with that obligation.
  5. Public interest: Where the processing is necessary for the performance of a task or function carried out in the interest of the public.
  6. Legitimate Interest: This means the interest of our business or that of our partners to enable us to give you the best services and most secure experience.
